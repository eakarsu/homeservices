  6. Free Cold Outreach

  Find leads for free:
  - Google Maps: Search "HVAC [your city]" → get business names/emails
  - Yelp: Browse contractor listings
  - Facebook: Find business pages, message them

  Email template (send from personal Gmail):
  Subject: Quick question about your dispatch software

  Hi [Name],

  I found [Company] on Google Maps. Quick question:
  are you using ServiceTitan or similar software?

  I built a much cheaper alternative ($99/month flat,
  no per-technician fees) with AI-powered dispatch.

  Looking for 5 contractors to try it free for 30 days.
  Interested in a quick look?

  [Your phone]

  Send 10-20 of these per day = free leads
