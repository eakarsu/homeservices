Complete Marketing Strategy for Your Field Service Software

  ---
  Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

  1. Create Your Marketing Assets

  | Asset               | Purpose                               |
  |---------------------|---------------------------------------|
  | Landing page        | Clear value prop, pricing, signup     |
  | Comparison pages    | "vs ServiceTitan", "vs Housecall Pro" |
  | Demo video          | 2-3 min product walkthrough           |
  | Case study template | For early customers                   |
  | Pricing page        | Transparent, no "contact sales"       |

  2. Define Your Positioning

  Target message:
  "The AI-powered field service platform that gives you ServiceTitan features at 1/10th the price. No per-technician fees. No contracts.
  Cancel anytime."

  Key differentiators to emphasize:
  - Flat pricing (not per-technician)
  - AI features included (not expensive add-ons)
  - No contracts, cancel anytime
  - Setup in hours, not weeks
  - Transparent pricing on website

  Phase 2: Low-Budget Launch Channels

  Where Contractors Actually Hang Out

  | Platform                   | Audience Size     | Strategy                              |
  |----------------------------|-------------------|---------------------------------------|
  | HVAC-Talk Forum            | 260,000+ members  | Participate genuinely, soft promotion |
  | r/HVAC                     | 140,000+ members  | Answer questions, share value         |
  | Contractor Talk            | 145,000+ members  | Engage in business/marketing threads  |
  | Plumbing Forums            | Active community  | Same approach                         |
  | HVAC Technician (Facebook) | 54,000+ members   | Share tips, not just pitches          |
  | ServiceTitan Masterminds   | Frustrated users! | Target unhappy customers              |

  Content Strategy for Forums

  DO:
  - Answer questions helpfully
  - Share genuinely useful tips
  - Mention your product only when relevant
  - Build reputation over time

  DON'T:
  - Spam links
  - Be salesy
  - Fake accounts

  Example post:
  "Hey everyone, I've been building field service software for a while and wanted to share some tips on optimizing dispatch routes...
  [value first]. By the way, I'm working on an affordable alternative to the big platforms if anyone's interested in trying it."

  Phase 4: Direct Outreach (Free/Low-Cost)

  Cold Email Strategy

  Target: Small HVAC/plumbing/electrical shops (5-20 employees)

  Subject lines:
  - "Tired of paying $500/tech for software?"
  - "ServiceTitan refugee?"
  - "[Name], quick question about your dispatch software"

  Email template:
  Hey [Name],

  I saw [Company] on Google and noticed you're in [City].

  Quick question: are you happy with your current
  dispatch/scheduling software? Or paying too much for it?

  I built an alternative to ServiceTitan that has AI-powered
  dispatch, scheduling, invoicing - everything you need -
  for a flat $99/month. No per-technician fees.

  Would you be open to a 15-min demo?

  [Your name]

  Where to Find Leads

  - Google Maps searches for "HVAC [city]"
  - Yelp business listings
  - BBB listings
  - Trade association directories
  - LinkedIn Sales Navigator
